2. Choose Recurring or Pre-Scheduled Alert Type and Create a New Alert Set

All Alert types require you to check-in with the app here, over SMS (at [1]415-800-1883), or over e-mail (checkin@safeworkproject.org').
A. Recurring Alert Set

A recurring alert set requires you to check-in with the app every pre-set time interval. For example, if you set it to require a check-in every 2 hours (120 minutes) and you activated it at 7:30PM, you would need to check-in at least once before 9:30PM, then again between 11:30PM, then 1:30AM, etc. until you miss a check-in and your alert is sent OR you deactivate the Alert Set.
